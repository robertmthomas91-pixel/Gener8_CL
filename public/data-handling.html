Data Handling Summary

Last updated: 20 June 2026
Template only. This document is a starting point and has not been reviewed by a lawyer. Have it reviewed by qualified legal counsel and tailored to your business before you rely on it.

A plain-English summary of how GENER8 handles your data, intended for prospective and current business customers. It complements the full Privacy Policy.

What is stored

  • Account records (email, hashed password, role, credits).
  • Your generations (prompts, output images/video/audio) and any reference images you upload.
  • Monthly usage counts per user, for administration and cost tracking.

Where it is stored

On our hosting provider's infrastructure (Railway), on a persistent volume. Generated media is served only to its owner and administrators.

Who can see it

Each user sees only their own feed. Administrators in your organisation can manage users and view usage totals, and can access stored media for support and moderation purposes.

How long it is kept

Generated media and inputs are automatically deleted after the configured retention window (default 30 days). Account data persists until the account is removed. Rolling backups of account data are kept for a limited period for disaster recovery.

Third parties

Prompts and uploaded images are sent to Google and ElevenLabs to perform generation. We do not use your content to train our own models. AI media may include provider watermarks (e.g. Google SynthID).

Deletion requests

You can delete individual items from your feed at any time. For full account deletion or a data export, contact your administrator or [CONTACT EMAIL].
